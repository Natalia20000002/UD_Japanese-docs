The dependency type `aux` is used for [auxiliary verbs](AUX) that attach to predicates.

~~~ sdparse
ご飯 を 食べ た 。 \n rice ACC eat PAST .
case(ご飯, を)
dobj(食べ, ご飯)
aux(食べ, た)
punct(食べ, 。)
~~~
