名詞 (もしくは代名詞) の形容詞的修飾句 (adjectival modifier) とは，名詞 (代名詞) の意味を修飾するような形容詞句を指す．

~~~ sdparse
Sam eats red meat
amod(meat, red)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Sam took out  a 3 million dollar loan
amod(loan, dollar)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Sam took out  a $ 3 million loan
amod(loan, $)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
There is nothing wrong with it
amod(nothing, wrong)
~~~
